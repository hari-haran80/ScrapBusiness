Metal Scrap :

Metal scrap refers to discarded metal materials that hold value because they can be recycled and reused. This category includes a range of metals:

  • Ferrous Metals: Iron, steel, and cast iron, known for their strength and magnetic properties, are commonly used in construction and manufacturing.
  • Non-Ferrous Metals: Metals like aluminum, copper, brass, and lead, which do not rust and are lighter, making them ideal for various industrial applications.
