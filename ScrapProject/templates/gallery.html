Metal Scraps

Aluminium Container

Aluminum food containers are lightweight, recyclable packaging materials commonly used for storing and transporting food. They are highly valued in the scrap industry for their reusability and eco-friendly nature

Aluminium Vessels

Aluminum vessels, commonly used in households for cooking and storage, are durable and corrosion-resistant. They hold significant value in the scrap industry due to their recyclability and potential for reuse in various applications

Copper Tube

Copper tubes are widely used in plumbing, refrigeration, and HVAC systems due to their durability and thermal conductivity. They are highly sought after in the scrap industry for their excellent recyclability and high resale value

Copper Wire

Copper wires are essential in electrical systems for their superior conductivity and flexibility. In the scrap industry, they are highly valuable for recycling, as copper retains its quality and fetches a good price when repurposed

Iron Mix

Iron scrap is a valuable material sourced from old machinery, construction debris, and industrial byproducts. It is widely recycled in the scrap industry, contributing to the production of new steel and reducing environmental impact

Iron Rod

Iron rods, commonly used in construction for reinforcement, provide strength and stability to concrete structures. In the scrap industry, these rods are recycled to create new materials, reducing waste and promoting sustainable building practices

Old Books

Old books are valuable in the scrap industry for their paper content, which can be recycled into new products. Recycling books helps reduce waste and promotes the sustainable use of resources while preserving the environment.

Plastic Chair

Plastic chairs, often used in households and public spaces, are durable but eventually wear out over time. In the scrap industry, they are recycled to reduce plastic waste and repurposed into new products, supporting a circular economy

Pc Water Bottles

PC (polycarbonate) water bottles are lightweight, durable containers commonly used for hydration. In the scrap industry, they are valuable for recycling, as polycarbonate can be repurposed into new plastic products, reducing environmental impact

PET Bottles

PET bottles are widely used for packaging beverages and other liquids due to their strength and lightweight nature. In the scrap industry, they are highly recyclable, helping to reduce plastic waste and support the production of new sustainable plastic products.

Mix Plastics

Mixed plastics consist of various types of plastic materials, often discarded together after use. In the scrap industry, they are collected and recycled into new products, helping to reduce plastic waste and promote environmental sustainability

Glucose Bottles

PVC Pipes

PVC pipes, commonly used in plumbing and construction, are durable and resistant to corrosion. In the scrap industry, they are highly recyclable, contributing to the production of new piping and reducing environmental waste
