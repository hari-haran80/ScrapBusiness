About Us

  • Welcome to Famous Scrap Business, a trusted leader in the scrap and recycling industry with over 25 years of experience. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to providing efficient, eco-friendly, and reliable scrap management solutions for individuals and businesses alike.
  • At Famous Scraps, we understand the importance of responsible recycling. Over the past two and a half decades, we’ve built a strong reputation based on trust, quality service, and a commitment to environmental sustainability. Our experienced team works with a wide range of scrap materials—metals, plastics, electronics, and more—ensuring that each item is responsibly recycled, reducing waste and promoting a cleaner, greener world.
  • Whether you're a small business, a large corporation, or an individual looking to recycle responsibly, we are here to meet your needs with professionalism and expertise. Join us in our mission to create a sustainable future, one piece of scrap at a time.

Our Mission

At Famous Scrap, our mission is to promote sustainable practices through efficient scrap collection and recycling. We are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service while maximizing the value of scrap materials. By turning waste into resources, we aim to reduce environmental impact, support local communities, and contribute to a cleaner planet.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in the recycling industry, recognized for our innovation and commitment to sustainability. We envision a future where waste is minimized, and every scrap material is treated as a valuable resource. Through education, technology, and community engagement, we strive to inspire others to join us in creating a circular economy that benefits the environment and society.
